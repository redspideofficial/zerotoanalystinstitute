Our Mission

-- Demystify Data --

To make data analysis easy, understandable, and less intimidating for everyone in the country, no matter their technical background.

-- Educate Individuals --

To guide individuals from "Zero to Analyst" through practical, hands-on learning and career development support, ensuring they are fully ready for real jobs.

-- Empower Business --

To equip Myanmar’s SMEs and corporate teams with strategic frameworks, custom dashboards, and consulting services that improve their operational decision-making.

-- Drive Success --

To transform raw data into actionable insights and real business success for organizations of all sizes, helping them achieve measurable, long-term results.

Our Vision

A future where data analysis is accessible to everyone in Myanmar, unlocking new opportunities in careers, business, and everyday life.

Our Goal

-- For Education --

To build a workforce that understands data, with a strong focus on career growth and personal improvement.

-- For Business --

To provide simple and accessible BI (Business Intelligence) and dashboard services so SMEs and startups can make decisions based on facts, not guesses.

-- For Culture --

To create a nationwide understanding that data analysis is an essential life skill that supports both career growth and daily decision-making.

What We Do...

We help career changers and motivated professionals build practical data analysis skills through hands-on, in-person training in Mandalay. Our classes are designed for students who want a strong foundation, whether they are starting from zero or improving their current skills. We also offer free career consultations by appointment to help future data analysts understand the real job role and plan their career path confidently. For businesses, we provide tailored SME and corporate services, including business analysis, strategic consulting, and custom dashboards to support smarter decisions and sustainable growth.
